--- RETURN POLICY ---
Our standard return policy allows customers to return any unused, unopened products within 30 days of the purchase date. To complete a return, you must present the original receipt or proof of purchase. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

--- REFUND PROCESSING TIME ---
Once a return is received and inspected at our warehouse, it takes between 5 to 7 business days to process your refund. Your bank or credit card provider might take an additional 2 to 3 days to post the transaction back to your account.

--- SHIPPING FEES ---
Standard shipping is free for all orders above $50. For orders under $50, a flat shipping fee of $5.99 is applied. Express 2-day shipping is available for all orders at a flat rate of $14.99.

--- CONTACT SUPPORT ---
If you need live support, you can reach our customer support team by emailing support@travelcompany.com or by calling 1-800-555-0199 between 9 AM and 5 PM EST, Monday through Friday.
